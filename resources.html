Recreational facilities are important as they provide a facility for physical activity, or recreation to take place. Here are some benefits of recreational facilities:

1. People tend to excercise more when they have access to a recreational facility

2. Recreational facilities have a positive impact on a community, as they provide a great space for mentorship programs to take place

3. Recreational facilities provides leadership development for the community youth

4. Recreational facilities keep the community safe, as it provides a location for at-risk youth to go, reducing crime rates

5. Clubs and fitness programs, athletic opportunities, and classes are made available by recreational centres
